Meme-Mashup

This meme mashup is based on Twilight and combines two different memes. I've used the meme generator to format the layout of these memes. The first is a scene featuring Jacob, who originally says, "Bella? Where have you been, loca?" I modified the text so that he's instead asking about Edward, another character in the movie. The second meme features actor Robert Pattinson, known for playing Edward in Twilight, standing awkwardly in a tracksuit. I merged this meme into a Batman setting, since Pattinson also plays Batman. I added Batman elements like his cape, mask, the Bat-Signal, and a Gotham City background. Jacob is looking for Edward, but instead of finding the Twilight character, he unknowingly stumbles upon Pattinson’s other major role—Batman. The joke is that Edward hasn’t just disappeared; he’s actually been in Gotham, fighting crime as the Batman The Dark Knight.

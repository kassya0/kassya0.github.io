Glitch

My series of glitched images features three beautiful women, distorted and rendered unrecognizable. In these glitches, I wanted to challenge the aesthetic of beauty by shifting from perfection and captivation to a chaotic mess, but in a way that it transforms into a untraditional type of beauty. In these images, they are Zendaya, Gigi Hadid and Maryilin Monroe.

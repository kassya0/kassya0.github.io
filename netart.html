NetArt

Using HTML and a text editor (I used Notepad++), we were tasked with creating a series of pages centered around a topic, story, or experience. The project required incorporating links, images, and pages to tell the story in a modern, hypertext, and poetic way. My story is an interactive narrative where players control the protagonist on a late-night journey home. Along the way, they must navigate through a series of choices that shape the direction of the story. The decisions made by the player lead to unique outcomes, making each playthrough different. It’s a classic adventure game filled with twists and turns, revealing hidden truths—if you can make it to the end. ​
